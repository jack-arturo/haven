# Haven Email Templates

## 1. Welcome Email (After Signup)

**Subject:** You're on the list — first drop launches soon

**Body:**
```
Hi there,

Welcome to Haven.

You're now on the list for weekly drop notifications. Every week, we launch one new product category — handmade by artisans around the world. Limited quantities. Once they're gone, they're gone.

**Week 1 launches in [X days]: Moroccan TV Covers**

Handwoven by Berber artisans in the Atlas Mountains. 4 patterns. 3 sizes. Only 15 units per combination.

We'll email you 24 hours before each drop goes live.

Reclaim your space,
The Haven Team

---

P.S. Want to know more about our philosophy? Read our story: [link to /about.html]
```
